Packing

We strive for continual improvement to enhance our productivity. Our main focus is to use best quality raw onions and garlic for dehydration. This ensures superior quality of dehydrated products.

We have qualified & experienced quality assurance personnel diligently conduct checks at every stage of production by carrying out mandatory microbiological tests. These tests are carried out periodically in government approved external laboratories.

Our products have a very low moisture content and perfect packaging thereby effectively inhibiting all deteriorative processes.

We have sophisticated raw material storage and cold storage facilities.

Quality is a source of Inspiration, satisfaction and pride. The team is committed to total customer satisfaction through consistent supply of reliable products in time. Customer orientation, adoption of state-of-the-art technologies, strategic planning and innovation are integral to its thought process.
